About

I am a startup enthusiast that does business development at Sauce Labs. I was previously the co-founder of Clothesliner and studied economics and political science at UCLA.

In 2011, I read How To Do What You Love by Paul Graham and began learning to program.

I play ultimate frisbee, ride my bicycle and reside in San Francisco, California. My favorite artists are Elvis Costello, P.G. Wodehouse, Kazuo Ishiguro, Andre Derain, Woody Allen, Wes Anderson and Belle & Sebastian.

Please feel free to contact me. I love to meet and hear from new people.
